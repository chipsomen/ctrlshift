STAR CLASSIFICATION

Stars are essential for the universe's existence, serving as the cosmic engines that drive its evolution. They produce light, heat, and energy, which are crucial for sustaining life on planets like Earth. Through the process of nuclear fusion, stars forge heavier elements that form the building blocks of planets, moons, and life itself. Their gravitational influence shapes the structure of galaxies and the cosmic web. Stars also play a role in the formation of planetary systems, including our own solar system. Without stars, the universe would be a vastly different and uninhabitable place.
