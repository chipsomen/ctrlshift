About Solar Eclipses

During a total solar eclipse, the Moon covers the Sun completely, making the sky dark for a short time. This reveals the Sun's outer atmosphere, called the corona. It's a rare event, and people travel to specfic places to see it because it's only visible from certain areas on Earth.
