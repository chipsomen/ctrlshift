There once was a man called Galileo Galilei, who revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos. By improving the telescope, he made groundbreaking discoveries, including the phases of Venus and Jupiter's moons, which supported the heliocentric model proposed by Copernicus. Said heliocentric model states that Earth and the rest of the planets revolve around the Sun at the center of the universe. Dive into the fruits of Galilei's efforts and the many astronomers after him with the click of a button.
